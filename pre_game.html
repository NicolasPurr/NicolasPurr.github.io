2.6 PRE-GAME CONFERENCE

Detailed Guide

A pre-game conference is essential for ensuring that all referees are aligned before the match. Key topics include areas of responsibility, positioning, and the use of the FIBA iRef Pre-Game App.

  • Know your areas of responsibility on the floor.
  • Establish eye contact for double calls.
  • Discuss help on out-of-bounds situations.

Download the FIBA iRef Pre-Game App from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.
