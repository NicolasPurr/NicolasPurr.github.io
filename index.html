Chapter 2: Individual Officiating Techniques

2.1 INTRODUCTION

Individual Officiating Techniques (IOT) is the most important foundation for either 2 Person Officiating (2PO) or 3 Person Officiating (3PO). Over the past 15 years, there has been a heavy focus on 3PO, which has led to a lack of knowledge in refereeing individual play phases.
