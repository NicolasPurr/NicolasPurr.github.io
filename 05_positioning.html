FIBA Referees Manual - Chapter 2: IOT

This chapter covers the benefits of maintaining an open angle and proper positioning on the court. A correct position maximizes the number of players in view, improving overall officiating accuracy.

  • Move to the right spot and establish the open angle.
  • Anticipate where you need to be.
  • Adjust your position to maintain the open angle.
