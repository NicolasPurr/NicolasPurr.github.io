# Alekhine Defense

![Alekhine Defense](https://www.thechesswebsite.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/alekhinedefensebig.jpg)

Alekhine's Defence is a chess opening that begins with the moves:

1. e4 Nf6
Black tempts White's pawns forward to form a broad pawn centre, with plans to undermine and attack the white structure later in the spirit of hypermodern defence.
