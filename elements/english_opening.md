# English Opening

![English Opening](https://www.thechesswebsite.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/EnglishOpening.jpg)

The English Opening is a chess opening that begins with the move:

1. c4
A flank opening, it is the fourth most popular and, according to various databases, one of the four most successful of White's twenty possible first moves.
