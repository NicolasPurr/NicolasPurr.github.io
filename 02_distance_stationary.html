2.2 DISTANCE & STATIONARY

FIBA Referees Manual - Chapter 2: IOT

This chapter explains the importance of maintaining the right distance from the play and remaining stationary. Many referees think that moving constantly helps decision-making. However, maintaining a distance of 3-6 meters allows you to see the play at a manageable speed.

  • Distance: The proper distance helps to reduce reaction calls and keeps the movements in perspective.
  • Stationary: Being stationary improves concentration and allows for a broader view of the play.
