2.7 MAKING A CALL, DECISION MAKING & COMMUNICATION

FIBA Referees Manual - Chapter 2: IOT

This chapter covers the importance of using proper whistle techniques, hand signals, and verbal communication when making decisions on the court.

  • Blow the whistle with a strong, short burst.
  • Release the whistle after making the call.
  • Indicate the decision with the appropriate signal.
  • Support your decision verbally.
  • Remember: less is more in communication.

For more details on what to do when not calling a play, see our Stay With the Play chapter.
